The dominant standard across the entire field: 1-Gas Fast Expand → Twilight Council → Adept Phase Shift Harass. Players take a single gas early, expand before the second gas, then build Twilight Council for Adept harass into the Terran's ramp. sOs deviates with an ultra-fast gas-less Nexus first. ShoWTimE and MaNa occasionally pivot to Stargate/Oracle.
